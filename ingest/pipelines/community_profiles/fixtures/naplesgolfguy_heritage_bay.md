# Heritage Bay
Heritage Bay is a bundled golf community with a 27-hole golf course. Since this is a bundled golf community, your golf membership is included in the purchase of your home or condo. Therefore, your golf dues are included with your regular community fees.

In bundled golf communities, the Golf or Social membership is included with the purchase of your home. Additionally, there are annual recurring fees associated with the property, as well as one-time fees due at the time of purchase.
Potential Fees Include:
  * Master HOA Fee (Annual)
  * Neighborhood HOA Fee (Annual)
  * Food and Beverage Minimum (Annual)
  * Assessments (Annual)
  * Capital Contribution Fee (one-time)
  * Transfer Fee (one-time)
  * Application Fee (one-time)
These fees vary by neighborhood within the community. Please feel free to contact us for additional information.
