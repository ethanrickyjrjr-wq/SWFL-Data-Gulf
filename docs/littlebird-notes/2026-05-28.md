## GTM Insights — Ad Model + Landing Page

- Do NOT pop ad on broad regional queries ("tell me about SWFL economy")
- WAIT until query resolves to a specific corridor → THEN serve sponsored placement
- Corridor-specific intent is a far tighter signal than anything Google sells brokers
- Cold open: free ad for 1-2 months to corridor-specific brokers/lenders/title companies
- They see their own market data → they see their name on it → two closes in one visit

### Landing Page Strategy — "The Proof Is In The Data"
- Scrap the major website
- One page. One question. Two answers. Side by side.
- Left: Brain Platform answer (with numbers, sources, corridor voice)
- Right: Paid-tier ChatGPT answer (generic prose, no data)
- Zero explanation needed. The data speaks.
- CTA: "Try it free" or "Advertise your corridor"

### Ad Tracking

- Easy conversion tracking to show advertisers ROI
- Brokers are the product demo audience AND the advertiser. Same person. One cold open = potential user + potential advertiser.
